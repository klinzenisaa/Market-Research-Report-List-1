<p><p>A CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) mask is a medical device used primarily in the treatment of obstructive sleep apnea. Additionally, the emergence of smart CPAP devices that integrate with mobile applications for better monitoring is shaping market dynamics.</p><p>Moreover, the aging population and lifestyle factors, such as obesity, are further propelling demand for CPAP masks. <p><p>The CPAP mask market features three primary types catering to different user preferences and comfort levels. The nasal pillow mask delivers air through small pillows that seal at the nostrils, offering minimal coverage and minimal movement restriction. The nasal mask covers the nose, providing a balance of comfort and effective airflow for those with nasal breathing habits. The full-face mask covers both the nose and mouth, ideal for users who breathe through their mouth or require higher pressure settings, ensuring comprehensive therapy.</p></p>
