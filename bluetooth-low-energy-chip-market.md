<p><ul><li>Enhanced Rate (EDR)</li><li>High Rate (HS/AMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) chip market can be categorized based on data transmission rates. Enhanced Data Rate (EDR) chips offer improved speeds, allowing for faster data transfers up to 3 Mbps, making them suitable for applications needing moderate bandwidth. High Speed (HS) chips, also known as AMP (Alternative MAC/PHY), combine Bluetooth with Wi-Fi to achieve even greater data rates, exceeding 20 Mbps. These classifications cater to various use cases, from fitness trackers to high-definition audio streaming.</p></p>
