<p><p>The Wireless EEG Collection System market is categorized primarily into three types: dry electrodes, wet electrodes, and saline electrodes. Dry electrodes are convenient and user-friendly, eliminating the need for conductive gel, making them ideal for mobile applications. Wet electrodes require conductive gel for optimal signal quality, ensuring high fidelity in recordings, but can be less comfortable. Saline electrodes use a saline solution to enhance conductivity, striking a balance between comfort and signal integrity, and are often used in specialized clinical settings.</p></p>
