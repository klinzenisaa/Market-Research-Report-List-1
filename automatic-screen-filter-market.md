Electric automatic screen filters utilize electric power for operation, offering precise control and automation, making them suitable for varied industrial applications. In contrast, hydraulic automatic screen filters rely on fluid pressure for cleaning and operation, making them ideal for environments where electricity may not be readily available. Both types enhance water filtration efficiency, ensuring consistent performance in various settings.</p></p>
