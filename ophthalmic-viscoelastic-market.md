<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersible Viscoelastic</li><li>Cohesive Viscoelastic</li><li>Double Viscoelastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic viscoelastic market comprises three main types: dispersible viscoelastic, cohesive viscoelastic, and double viscoelastic. Dispersible viscoelastic agents are designed to spread easily and remove cellular debris, enhancing visibility during surgery. Cohesive viscoelastic agents, on the other hand, maintain their shape and provide support for the eye's structure, facilitating surgical manipulation. Double viscoelastic combines the properties of both types, offering optimal performance by facilitating both cleaning and structural support, making it versatile for various ophthalmic procedures.</p></p>
