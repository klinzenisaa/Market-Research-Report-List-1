<p><p>The wireless ANC headphone market comprises three main types: Full Cup (Circumaural), On-Ear (Supraaural), and In-Ear (Intraaural). Full Cup headphones enclose the ears completely, providing excellent noise isolation and comfort for long listening sessions. On-Ear headphones rest directly on the ears, offering a balance between portability and sound quality. In-Ear headphones fit snugly within the ear canal, making them compact and ideal for active use, often featuring customizable ear tips for enhanced noise cancellation and fit.</p></p>
