Online AOI machines are integrated into production lines, providing real-time inspection and feedback, enhancing efficiency and reducing downtime. In contrast, offline AOI machines function independently, allowing for batch inspections away from the production process, suitable for quality control and final checks. Both types aim to ensure product quality in manufacturing, particularly in electronics, by detecting defects and ensuring adherence to specifications.</p></p>
