<p><p>The industrial metal 3D printer market features several key technologies. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) utilizes high-powered lasers to fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating complex geometries with excellent mechanical properties. Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, ideal for high-temperature materials and producing dense parts. Other market types include binder jetting and direct energy deposition, which offer alternative methods for metal additive manufacturing, catering to specific industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
