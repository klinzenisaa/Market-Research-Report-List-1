<p><p>The neurointerventional equipment market includes various types of procedures aimed at treating vascular issues in the brain. Neurothrombectomy involves removing clots from brain vessels to restore blood flow. Cerebral angiography is a diagnostic imaging technique used to visualize blood vessels in the brain. Stenting involves placing a stent to keep vessels open, preventing narrowing. Coiling procedures utilize coils to occlude aneurysms, reducing the risk of rupture. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing neurovascular care and patient outcomes.</p></p>
