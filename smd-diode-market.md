<p><p>The SMD diode market is segmented into several types, each serving unique functions. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching speeds, ideal for power applications. Transient Suppression Diodes (TVS) protect circuits against voltage spikes. Rectifier diodes convert AC to DC, widely used in power supplies. Other diodes include varieties like Zener and light-emitting diodes, catering to specific applications in voltage regulation and lighting. Collectively, these diodes are essential in modern electronic designs.</p></p>
