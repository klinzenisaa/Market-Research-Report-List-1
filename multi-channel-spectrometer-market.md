<p><p>The multi-channel spectrometer market is categorized into three main types: UV (ultraviolet), VL (visible light), and NIR (near-infrared). UV spectrometers are used for analyzing substances that absorb ultraviolet light, making them vital in chemical and biological applications. VL spectrometers focus on the visible spectrum and are commonly used for environmental monitoring and material analysis. NIR spectrometers detect near-infrared light, often employed in agricultural and food industry for quality control and composition analysis, leveraging unique absorption properties of substances.</p></p>
