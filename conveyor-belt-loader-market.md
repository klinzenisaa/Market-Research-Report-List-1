<p><p>The conveyor belt loader market is categorized into two main types: self-propelled and towable models. Self-propelled conveyor belt loaders are equipped with their own power source and mobility, enabling them to operate independently at airports and cargo facilities. Towable models, on the other hand, require a towing vehicle for movement but are typically more versatile and cost-effective for transporting cargo. Both types facilitate efficient loading and unloading processes, enhancing operational productivity in logistics and aviation sectors.</p></p>
