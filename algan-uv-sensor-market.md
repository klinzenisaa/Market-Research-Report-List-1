<p><p>The AlGaN UV sensor market comprises three main types: UVA sensors, UVB sensors, and UVC sensors, each tailored for specific applications. UVA sensors detect wavelengths from 320 to 400 nm, primarily used in skin care and environmental monitoring. UVB sensors, sensitive to 290 to 320 nm, are crucial for assessing UV radiation exposure and its effects on human health and crops. UVC sensors, operating in the 200 to 290 nm range, are vital for disinfection and sterilization applications, particularly in healthcare and water treatment.</p></p>
