<p><p>The infrared thermal cameras market is segmented into three main types based on wavelength: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR cameras excel in visibility through smoke and fog, making them ideal for surveillance and industrial applications. MWIR cameras are known for their high-resolution capabilities, primarily used in defense and aerospace. LWIR cameras are widely utilized in building inspections and energy audits, as they detect heat patterns effectively, crucial for identifying energy losses.</p></p>
