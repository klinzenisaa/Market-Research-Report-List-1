<p><p>The Angiographic Interventional Therapy System market encompasses various imaging techniques for vascular assessment. X-ray Angiography is widely used for real-time imaging of blood vessels, providing detailed information during interventions. CT Angiography offers cross-sectional images, enhancing visualization of complex structures. Magnetic Resonance Angiography utilizes magnetic fields to produce high-resolution images without ionizing radiation, ideal for soft tissue evaluation. MR-CT Angiography combines both modalities, leveraging the strengths of each to deliver comprehensive vascular imaging for improved diagnostic and therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
