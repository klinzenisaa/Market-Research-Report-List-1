<p><strong>The EC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EC motor market is primarily segmented into two types: inner rotor motors and outer rotor motors. Inner rotor motors feature the rotor inside the stator, facilitating more compact designs and higher speeds, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and space efficiency. In contrast, outer rotor motors have the rotor encasing the stator, offering greater torque at lower speeds and enhanced cooling. Both types are widely used in automotive, HVAC, and industrial applications, reflecting their versatile performance characteristics.</p></p>
