<p><ul><li>On Premises</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud manufacturing software can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing flexible access, lower costs, and automatic updates. This model promotes collaboration and scalability, making it ideal for businesses seeking to innovate rapidly and efficiently adapt to market demands while minimizing IT burdens.</p></p>
