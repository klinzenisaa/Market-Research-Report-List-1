<p><strong>The Epoxy Adhesive for Electronics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Multi-component Epoxy Adhesive"</li><li>"One-Component Epoxy Adhesive"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy adhesive market for electronics is divided into two main types: multi-component and one-component epoxy adhesives. Multi-component epoxy adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener, which must be mixed before application, offering strong bonding capabilities and customizable curing times. One-component epoxy adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, simplifying the application process and storage but typically requiring heat or specific conditions to cure. Both types are crucial for secure, durable electronic component assembly and repair.</p></p>
