<p><strong>The Drilling & Completion Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based systems</li><li>Oil-based systems</li><li>Synthetic-based systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling and completion fluids market comprises three primary types: water-based systems, oil-based systems, and synthetic-based systems. Water-based systems are environmentally friendly and cost-effective, utilizing water as the primary base fluid. Oil-based systems, offering superior lubrication and reducing friction, are ideal for challenging formations but pose environmental concerns. Synthetic-based systems combine high performance with lower environmental impact, using synthetic fluids to enhance efficiency and wellbore stability. Each type serves unique operational needs in drilling activities.</p></p>
