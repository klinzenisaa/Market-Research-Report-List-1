<p><strong>The Residential Solar PV Inverters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>String Inverters</li><li>Micro Inverters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential solar PV inverter market is primarily divided into two types: string inverters and micro inverters. String inverters connect multiple solar panels in series, optimizing performance for entire arrays and offering a cost-effective solution. They are generally easier to install and maintain. In contrast, micro inverters are attached to individual panels, allowing for better energy management and performance optimization, especially in shaded conditions. This decentralized approach enhances overall efficiency and increases energy harvest, though typically at a higher up-front cost.</p></p>
