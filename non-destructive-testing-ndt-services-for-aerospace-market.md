<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services in the aerospace market encompass various techniques to evaluate materials without causing damage. Visual inspections assess surface conditions, while liquid penetrant testing identifies surface cracks. Acoustic emission testing detects active defects through sound waves, and leak testing checks for fluid leaks in components. Radiography uses X-rays for internal inspection, while ultrasonic testing employs sound waves for thickness measurement. Magnetic particle testing reveals surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials, and eddy-current testing detects conductive material defects. Other methods may include advanced techniques tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
