<p><ul><li>Tankless Propane Water Heaters</li><li>Tank Propane Water Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propane water heaters market includes two main types: tankless and tank propane water heaters. Tankless propane water heaters provide on-demand hot water, heating water directly as needed, which increases energy efficiency and saves space. They are ideal for smaller homes or for users wanting continuous hot water. In contrast, tank propane water heaters store a large volume of heated water in a tank, ensuring immediate access but often consuming more energy due to standby heat loss. Each type suits different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
