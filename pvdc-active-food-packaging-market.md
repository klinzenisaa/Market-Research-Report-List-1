<p><p>The PVDC Active Food Packaging Market is characterized by two main types: PVDC Latex and PVDC Resin. PVDC Latex is a flexible, water-based coating that enhances barrier properties and improves adhesion in food packaging applications, ensuring the freshness and extending the shelf life of products. PVDC Resin, on the other hand, is a solid material used for manufacturing films and containers, offering superior moisture and gas barrier characteristics. Both types are crucial for maintaining food quality and safety throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
