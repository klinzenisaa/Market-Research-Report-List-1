<p><p>The artificial abrasive market primarily consists of two key types: corundum and silicon carbide abrasives. Corundum abrasives, derived from aluminum oxide, are highly durable and effective in metalworking and finishing applications. Silicon carbide abrasives, on the other hand, are known for their sharpness and are ideal for harder materials, including glass and ceramics. Both types cater to different industries, offering unique properties that enhance grinding, cutting, and polishing processes, thus driving their demand in the manufacturing sector.</p></p>
