<p><strong>The Electrical Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step Up Transformer & Step Down Transformer</li><li>Three Phase Transformer & Single Phase Transformer</li><li>Two Winding Transformer & Auto Transformer</li><li>Outdoor Transformer & Indoor Transformer</li><li>Oil Cooled & Dry Type Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical transformer market comprises several types, including step-up transformers, which increase voltage, and step-down transformers, which decrease voltage. Three-phase transformers provide efficient power distribution for industrial applications, while single-phase transformers are suited for residential use. Two winding transformers have distinct primary and secondary coils, whereas auto transformers share a common winding. Outdoor transformers are designed for external use, while indoor transformers are built for sheltered environments. Oil-cooled transformers use oil for cooling, while dry-type transformers rely on air for insulation and cooling.</p></p>
