Marketing cooperatives help dairy farmers collectively market their products, ensuring better pricing and access to larger markets. Federated cooperatives unite multiple local cooperatives, enhancing bargaining power and resource sharing, ultimately benefiting members by pooling resources for more effective operations and marketing strategies.</p></p>
