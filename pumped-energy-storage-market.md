<p><p>Pumped Energy Storage (PES) is a method of storing energy by using electricity to pump water from a lower elevation to a higher elevation. During periods of high electricity demand, the water is released back to the lower elevation, generating hydroelectric power. This technology offers a reliable way to balance supply and demand, especially with the growing integration of intermittent renewable energy sources, such as wind and solar.</p><p>The Pumped Energy Storage Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the increasing need for grid stability and energy security.
