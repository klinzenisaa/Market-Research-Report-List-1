<p><p>Clean coal technologies encompass several market types aimed at reducing emissions and enhancing efficiency in coal utilization. Supercritical pulverised coal combustion operates at high pressure and temperature, improving thermal efficiency. Fluidised bed combustion utilizes a mixture of air and coal particles, promoting uniform burning and lower emissions. Gasification converts coal into syngas, which can be used for power generation and chemical production, thus minimizing pollutants. Other technologies may include carbon capture and storage, advanced combustion processes, and integrated gasification combined cycle systems.</p></p>
